Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 20, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to KeyVault. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it. This applies to all information collected through our website and related services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the website, express an interest in obtaining information about us or our products, or when you participate in activities on the website. This includes:

  • Account Data: Name, email address, and encrypted passwords.
  • Transaction Data: Details about payments you make and digital keys you purchase (e.g., product name, timestamp).
  • Device Data: IP addresses, browser type, and operating system for security and fraud prevention.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, improve, and secure our services. Specific uses include:

  • To facilitate account creation and the logon process.
  • To fulfill and manage your orders and payments.
  • To send verification codes (OTP) and order delivery emails.
  • To protect our services against bot attacks and fraudulent transactions.

4. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. We use industry-standard encryption for data at rest and in transit. Your digital keys are stored in a secured database and are only accessible after successful authentication.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your experience. These are small files that a site or its service provider transfers to your computer's hard drive through your web browser that enables the site's systems to recognize your browser and capture/remember certain information (like items in your cart).

6. Third-Party Services

We may share information with third-party service providers to perform functions on our behalf, such as:

  • Payment processing (via encrypted cryptocurrency gateways).
  • Cloud hosting and image storage (e.g., Cloudinary).
  • Customer support tools.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can manage most of your data directly through your profile page. To request a full account deletion, please contact our support team.

Privacy Questions?

If you have any questions about this policy, please reach out to our privacy officer at privacy@keyvault.com or visit our Contact Page.
